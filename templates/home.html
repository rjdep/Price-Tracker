PriceTracker

Free Price Tracker For Flipkart

About ::

When it comes to online shopping, prices are always fluctuating. So, working out when to buy a product at the best rate you need to constantly keep an eye on its price. And, it gets worse when you find out that you’ve missed out on a great deal.
So, instead of monitoring price changes manually, you can use this Flipkart Price Tracker which will do all the monitoring of prices of the products available on Flipkart only. (Although, In the future, we may expand it to other e-commerce websites ). The app will monitor the price changes every 30 seconds so that you don't miss out any great deal.

ADDITION OF PRODUCT TRACKER:
Click on the "Add" button to add the product which you are looking for. Copy the CORRECT product's URL, CORRECT means the URL of the webpage which contains the "Add to Cart/ Buy Now" option from the Flipkart Website. Paste the link on the URL's tab, and set an upper bound of the amount of the product.
Note: At max you can add {{numMax}} products at a time.

PRICE DROP ALERTS
Price Tracker alerts you when price of a product that you are tracking is under your set target by sending a notifications to your registered Email ID.
Note: Once the Email is sent the product will automatically get removed from the tracker list.

CANCELLATION OF PRODUCT TRACKER:
The process of cancellation is very easy. Visit the "My Url List" and click on the "cancel Tracking" .
